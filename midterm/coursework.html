CLASSE(S) I'VE TAKEN

  • Digital Design and Storytelling - DMA102

    Instructor: Adrienne Von Wolffersdorff

What did you know before this class?

Some of the things I knew in this class was generally how any story would be structured. I wasn't a complete expert, but given my years of analyzing films or videos I can fairly say that I had a good idea of how a buildup, an important event occurs that changes the normality, a progression, a falling action, a solution to that falling action, and the resolve at happens at the end of the story.

What knowledge/skills did you gain after this class?

This class has opened my range of imagination when creating a story. In terms of knowledge and skills, this class has taught me to keep a more sharp eye and pay attention to more details not only in film but also in real life situations.
