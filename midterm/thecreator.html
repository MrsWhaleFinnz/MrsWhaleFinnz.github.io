A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Photo taken at 6:39P.M.

Hello everyone! It's nice to meet you all!

My name is Kenny Yu, I'm currently thinking about what my gender identity is (most likely a she), and I'm 25 years old and attending Bellevue College for my Digital Media Arts Degree (and more if my current field of interest requires it.)

I am a Self-Taught 2D Digital Artist and a 3D Artist, but attending classes on 3D Design to enhance my current skills. I am passionate about video games and I'm taking classes so that I can become a Game Designer/Developer. What I plan to do is to become someone who can lead a team of other game designers so that I can produce my video game ideas to the world, while also owning my own online merch store, creating 3D models for other individuals as a side gig.

I'm into digital art, figurines, anime merch gaming, watching films, going shopping and having fun.
